Winooski Walking Routes

Maximize your Pokemon Adventures in Winooski, VT.

Efficient Pokestop Loop

10-15 minutes per cycle

This is the most efficient method of grabbing the 10 stop bonus in Winooski. The route bypasses all traffic and is thus the fastest possible route in the area. Be sure to walk up the ramp by the Champlain Mill stop so you can also hit the Winooski Falls Bus Stop, or walk north to hit Winooski One before crossing back under the bridge.

Efficient Pokemon Loop

25-35 minutes per cycle

This route maximizes the volume of Pokemon you encounter on each loop. A good portion of Pokemon spawn at the far end of Champlain Mill Path (past Green Man). Another mass spawning area is the walkway from Cascade Way to Abenaki Way (towards CCV). Additional water spawns and the Champlain Mill cluster are encountered along the route twice to maximize Pokemon / minute.

The "Rare Spawns" Loop

30-40 minutes per cycle

This route brings you past all rare spawn points in the downtown Winooski area. Note this route is fairly similar to the Efficient Pokemon Loop, except it extends further north to reach another 5-6 rare Pokemon spawn points (specifically near Winooski City Hall and McKee's Irish Pub). Traffic volume will exponentially increase loop completion time, and is not recommended during rush hour traffic.
